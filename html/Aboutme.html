About Me

Welcome to My Life

I am 22 years old and was born in Idaho Falls, Idaho. I am an avid sports fan and love playing and watching sports. I also love being active and doing things outdoors. Some of my favorite outdoor hobbies include skiing, dirt biking, hiking, and sight seeing. I love traveling and want to someday travel the world.

Watch this amazing skiing video!

I gained an interest in finance while I was in highschool taking a economics/finance class. Basic principles such as the time value of money grabbed my attention and I also was intrigued by the stock market. As I learned more about finance, I realized that many people struggle with managing their finances and I want to help them. I know that everyone works hard to make a living and a lot of people don't understand how to manage the money they are making. I hope to help others so they can reduce the stress and worry in their lives and enjoy being with their families and loved ones.
